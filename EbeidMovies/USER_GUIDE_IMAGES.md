# Quick Start Guide: Using Image Features in EbeidMovies

## How to Add a Movie with a Poster Image

1. **Open the App** and tap the floating **[+]** button
2. **Fill in Movie Details**:
   - Movie Title (required)
   - Director (required)
   - Release Year (required)
   - Genre (required)
   - Notes (optional)

3. **Add Movie Poster** (optional):
   - Scroll down to "Movie Poster (Optional)" section
   - Tap on the dashed box area
   - Your device's image picker will open
   - Select an image from your gallery
   - The image will preview in the form

4. **Change Image** (if needed):
   - Tap the "Change Image" button below the preview
   - Select a different image

5. **Save**: Tap "Add Movie to Watchlist"

## Viewing Movies with Images

### In the List View:
- Each movie card shows a small poster thumbnail (80x80 pixels) on the left
- Movies without posters show a gray film icon placeholder
- You can still check/uncheck watched status
- Tap the trash icon to delete

### In the Detail View:
- Tap any movie in the list to see full details
- The full poster image appears at the top (if available)
- Scroll down to see complete movie information
- All CRUD operations work the same (mark watched, delete)

## Troubleshooting

### "Can't select image"
- Make sure you granted photo permissions
- Check Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí EbeidMovies ‚Üí Permissions

### "Image not showing"
- Image might be loading (wait a moment)
- Try selecting the image again
- Restart the app

### "Image looks blurry"
- Use higher resolution images for best quality
- Recommended: 300x450px or larger for posters

## Permissions Explained

The app requests:
- **READ_MEDIA_IMAGES** (Android 13+): Access to select photos
- **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE** (older Android): Access to gallery

These permissions are ONLY used when you choose to add movie posters. Your privacy is protected.
